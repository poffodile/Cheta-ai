Introduction

Cheta is an AI-powered personal assistant designed to help people remember what matters, turn intentions into actions, and continuously grow through guidance, planning, and reflection.

Cheta was born from a simple problem.

As a Software Engineering student, I found myself using multiple disconnected tools to manage different parts of my life. I had one application for tasks, another for notes, another for reminders, another for goals, and I would often rely on ChatGPT for advice, planning, project ideas, interview preparation, CV reviews, and countless other things.

Although these tools were individually useful, I realised that nothing truly connected everything together.

There was no system that remembered what I had been working on last week, what projects I wanted to build, what goals I had set for myself, or what opportunities I should be pursuing next.

Many times, I knew what I wanted to achieve, but turning those intentions into actions was difficult. I would forget things, lose track of ideas, postpone tasks, and struggle to maintain consistency.

I wanted something that could bring all of these things together in one place.

I wanted something that could:

* Remember previous conversations.
* Help me organise my life.
* Suggest projects.
* Help with interviews.
* Remind me to update my CV.
* Suggest LinkedIn posts.
* Break goals into smaller tasks.
* Track habits and progress.
* Help me reflect on achievements.
* Provide guidance when I felt overwhelmed.
* Grow alongside me.

Over time, the vision expanded beyond being just a planner or a chatbot.

I started imagining something much bigger.

Not simply an AI assistant, but an intelligent companion that understands context, remembers important information, and helps transform aspirations into consistent action.

⸻

Why The Name “Cheta”

The name “Cheta” comes from the Igbo language and means “remember.”

Choosing this name was deeply personal.

As someone proud of my roots, I wanted the product to carry part of my identity and culture while remaining simple and easy to pronounce internationally.

The name perfectly reflects the core purpose of the product.

Cheta remembers.

It remembers goals.

It remembers conversations.

It remembers projects.

It remembers achievements.

It remembers what matters.

But remembering alone is not enough.

Cheta exists to help people act on those memories and transform them into meaningful progress.
