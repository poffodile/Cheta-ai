Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Product Overview

Cheta is an AI-powered personal companion built to help you stay on point — remembering what matters, planning properly, and actually getting things done.

Think of Cheta as your second brain. It keeps track of your ideas, goals, projects, and progress, while guiding you with AI so you don’t fall behind. It’s built for Martin and anyone trying to level up and actually see their growth in real time.

Product Goals

Cheta is here to:

* Keep users organised and locked in.
* Stop important things from slipping.
* Give real, useful AI guidance (not waffle).
* Turn goals into clear, actionable steps.
* Push consistent growth.
* Bring everything into one place — projects, planning, career moves, and reflection.
